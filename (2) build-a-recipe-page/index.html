Lasagna Recipe

The recipe for you to make the best and tastiest lasagna of your life!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat; drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, basil, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  7. Add half of the ground beef, followed by a layer of marinara sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
